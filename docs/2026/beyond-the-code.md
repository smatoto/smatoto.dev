## Abstract

As AI rapidly reshapes software development, upcoming graduates face both exciting opportunities and new challenges. This talk will equip attendees with the adaptable mindset and core technical skills needed to confidently collaborate with AI and build the future of tech without limits. The session explores how to transition from writing basic code to architecting intelligent, resilient systems that solve real-world problems. Ultimately, participants will learn how to leverage continuous learning and innovation to stand out and lead in the modern IT industry.


## Outline

- The new landscape: how AI is changing what it means to be a developer
- From writing code to architecting intelligent systems
- The mindset shift: collaborating with AI, not competing with it
- Core technical skills that remain foundational in an AI-first world
- Building resilient, scalable systems that solve real problems
- Continuous learning as a career strategy - not just a habit

## Key Takeaways

- AI is a collaborator, not a replacement - developers who embrace it will lead
- The shift from coder to architect is the most important career transition in tech right now
- Systems thinking - scalability, resilience, and good design - is more valuable than ever
- Continuous learning is not optional; it's the defining skill of the modern engineer
- The best way to stand out is to build things that matter, not just things that work
